## How to Play

- **Tap** an empty cell to place a `/` mirror
- **Tap again** to rotate it to a `\` mirror
- **Tap once more** to remove it
- Fixed glowing crystals are pre-placed and cannot be moved
- All receivers must be lit with the **matching color** to clear the level
