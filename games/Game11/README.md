# Swap Path 🏹

An arrow-grid puzzle. Every cell holds an arrow. The walker starts at the **START** cell and follows arrows one step at a time. Collect every **★ star** before stepping onto the **EXIT**.

## How to Play

- **Tap two non-fixed cells** to swap their arrows (costs 1 swap)
- Fixed cells (bright border) cannot be swapped
- Press **Walk** to simulate the walker's path
- If the path loops, goes out of bounds, or misses a star — press Reset and try again

## Tips

- Trace the default path mentally before swapping
- Swaps are limited — plan carefully
- One good swap can completely redirect the path

## Levels

8 levels of increasing complexity with up to 3 stars to collect.
